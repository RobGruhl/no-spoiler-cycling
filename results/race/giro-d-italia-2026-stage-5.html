Stage 5: Praia a Mare → Potenza
Giro d'Italia 2026

Igor Arrieta took UAE's second stage win in two days in a rain-affected hilly stage to Potenza, beating Afonso Eulálio in a small front-group finale. Eulálio's second place plus stage 4 lead time gave him the pink jersey going into the Naples sprint stage.

§ 01 · The stage

Arrieta wins the rain-stage; Eulálio into pink

~203 km from Praia a Mare to Potenza, hilly with rain-affected chaos from the start of the stage. The race settled into a small front-group finale that Arrieta won; Eulálio rolled in second, Scaroni third.

§ 02 · GC impact

Afonso Eulálio in pink

The Portuguese climber's second place plus accumulated GC lead from the Bulgarian opening puts him in the maglia rosa.
