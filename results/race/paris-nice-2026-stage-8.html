§ 01 · The stage

Lenny Martinez wins stage, Vingegaard wins GC

143 km, hilly Nice circuit, the Côte du Linguador as the day's decisive climb. Vingegaard and Lenny Martinez gapped the chasers on the climb and rode the final kilometres two-up; Martinez took the stage, Vingegaard the overall by more than four minutes.

§ 02 · GC impact

Vingegaard wins Paris-Nice 2026

Vingegaard the overall winner — career-rebooting GC win, his first major after the 2024 Itzulia crash. Daniel Martínez 2nd at +4:23, Steinhauser third.
