§ S8 · HILLY · 156 km

Stage 8: Chieti → Fermo
Giro d'Italia 2026

Jhonatan Narváez took UAE's second stage win in five days, a solo ride into Fermo's hill-circuit muri after teammate Mikkel Bjerg's late attack opened the gap. Leknessund 2nd at +32, Tjøtta 3rd at +42 for an Uno-X double. The day Vingegaard's leader's jersey defended itself — no GC threats in the break.

§ 01 · The stage

Narváez wins again, UAE rebuild continues

156 km from Chieti to Fermo. First 100 km uncomplicated; the closing 55 km dense with the Muri in Le Marche — Montefiore d'Aso KOM at ~60 km to go, Capodarco (2.5 km at 6.1%), the steep final wall climb back into Fermo inside the closing 10 km.

The stage played out as everyone expected: breakaway day, GC peloton content to ride tempo behind. The pre-final-circuit break thinned across the four closing climbs to a trio of Narváez, Bjerg (both UAE) and Leknessund. Bjerg attacked at 10 km to go on the climb back to Fermo — a deliberate UAE 1-2 move; Leknessund couldn't hold the Dane's pace. Narváez then went over the top of Bjerg's effort and rode the closing kilometres solo to the line. Leknessund 32 seconds back, Tjøtta + the next chase 42 seconds.

§ 02 · GC impact

Vingegaard defends without spending

GC unchanged on a day designed for the break. The Blockhaus margins from Stage 7 hold; the GC question stays open between Vingegaard and Gall heading into the second-week climbing stages.
