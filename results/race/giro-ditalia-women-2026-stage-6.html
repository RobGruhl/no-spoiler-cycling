Giro d'Italia Women 2026

§ 01 · The stage

Balsamo's fourth win in a technical Brescello sprint

The 160 km flat-to-rolling stage from Ala to Brescello returned the race to the sprinters, but not without drama. An early four-rider Italian break (Bezzone, Spimi, Segato, Cagnazzo) reached around four minutes before being reeled in. Mid-stage, Uno-X drove a split in the peloton: van der Breggen made the front group, but Vollering and Reusser were briefly caught behind before Movistar and UAE chased it back together with no decisive gap.

In the finale, Giorgia Serena soloed about a minute clear with 12 km to go before being caught. With only Lucinda Brand left as a lead-out, Lidl - Trek still produced a long pull through the twisting closing kilometre — Movistar even briefly turned the wrong way inside the final km. Balsamo launched from around fifth wheel and won decisively for her fourth stage win of the race (the first awarded after Wiebes's DQ, plus Stages 2, 3 and 6 on the road).

§ 02 · GC impact

No GC change; van der Breggen retains pink

GC after Stage 6 was unchanged from Stage 5, all contenders finishing in the bunch: van der Breggen in pink, Vollering 2nd at +1:00, Niedermaier 3rd at +1:24, Holmgren 4th at +2:01, Reusser 5th at +2:03, Longo Borghini 6th at +2:12, Fisher-Black 7th at +2:33. Balsamo extended her points-jersey lead with the win and an intermediate-sprint bonus.
