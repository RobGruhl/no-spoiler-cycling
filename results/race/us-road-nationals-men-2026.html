§ 01 · The race in 90 seconds

Simmons goes solo again for a third stars-and-stripes

OPENINGThe elite men covered 198.6 km on the hilly Charleston circuit, the steep Wertz Avenue climb shredding the bunch lap after lap and shaping a strong lead group of WorldTour and domestic riders.

UNFOLDSRepeated efforts on Wertz Avenue distilled the race to a select front group, with Lidl-Trek, UAE Team Emirates XRG and Tudor all represented and the climb doing the selecting.

DECIDEDOn the Wertz Avenue ascent with roughly 29 km remaining — almost the exact spot he has launched before — Quinn Simmons attacked alone and immediately opened a gap the chasers could not close.

FINALESimmons time-trialled the run-in solo to take the title, while behind him Kevin Vermaerke jumped the chase sprint to edge Lawrence Warbasse for second.
