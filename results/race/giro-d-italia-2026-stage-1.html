§ 01 · The stage

Magnier takes Stage 1 and the pink

147 km along the Bulgarian Black Sea coast from Nessebar to Burgas, finishing on two laps of a short city circuit. A late crash inside the final 3 km meant same-time bunch credit for GC; the front of the sprint was contested clean. Magnier — Soudal's 22-year-old French sprinter — claimed his first Grand Tour stage and the first maglia rosa of the 2026 Giro.

§ 02 · GC impact

Magnier in pink

First leader's jersey to Magnier on time bonuses. The opening trio of stages in Bulgaria were always going to be a holding period for GC; the climbing returned with Stage 7.
