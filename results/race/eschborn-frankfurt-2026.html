§ 01 · The race in 90 seconds

Zimmermann at home, Pidcock and Tulett complete the podium

OPENING211 km from Eschborn into Frankfurt, the German May Day staple. A reduced 12-rider front group survived clear of the peloton.

UNFOLDSStandard Eschborn pattern — the climbing into Königstein-area trims the bunch, the front group establishes itself, the peloton's chase doesn't quite close.

DECIDEDInside the closing kilometre, the 12-up sprint goes to the rider with the best closing kick and the local crowd. Zimmermann had both.

FINALEZimmermann won from the back of the group, sprinting along the barriers. Pidcock second, Tulett third — a clean podium spread.
