Stage 1: Bilbao Opening
Itzulia Basque Country 2026

Paul Seixas took the 13.8 km hilly ITT in Bilbao by 23 seconds over Vauquelin and 27 over Großschartner — the 19-year-old becoming the youngest WorldTour stage race leader of the year. Ayuso lost 1:16 in a defining early GC hit.

§ 01 · The stage

Seixas takes the leader's jersey

Hilly 13.8 km ITT. Seixas posted the fastest time of the day, beating every billed GC favourite. Roglič 4th at +28; Großschartner the second man ever to defeat Roglič at an Itzulia TT.
