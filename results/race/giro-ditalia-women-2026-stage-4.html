§ 01 · The stage

Van der Breggen demolishes the field in the Nevegal TT

The first true GC day was a 12.7 km uphill time trial from Belluno to Nevegal, and Anna van der Breggen turned it into a statement. She was already 36 seconds up on Vollering at the first intermediate split and only extended her advantage on the upper slopes, stopping the clock at 31:38.

Marlen Reusser, the TT world champion, rode the discipline she dominates but could not match the climber — even after starting on a road bike and accelerating as the gradients steepened, she finished 1:04 down. Vollering went out fast, quicker than Reusser at the first checkpoint, but faded on the climb to come home 1:10 back in third.

The result transformed the race. After three days of Balsamo sprints with the GC bunched, van der Breggen took the maglia rosa with a real cushion over every rival, and the time-trial gaps would frame the GC battle all the way to the final weekend.

§ 02 · GC impact

Van der Breggen takes pink with a commanding lead

Because it was an individual time trial, the stage result equals the GC: van der Breggen in pink, Reusser 2nd at +1:04, Vollering 3rd at +1:10, Niedermaier 4th at +1:26, Trinca Colonel 5th at +1:31, Dickson +1:38, de Vries +1:39, Longo Borghini +1:51. Balsamo, the previous leader, dropped out of GC contention on the climb.
