Tour de Romandie
2026

Dorian Godon won the 3.2 km Villars-sur-Glâne prologue for INEOS Grenadiers, with Ivo Oliveira and Jakob Søderqvist tied for second at +6 seconds. Pogačar — the overall race favourite — opened a distant sixth at +7, deliberately conservative on a technical short course before the climbing days. § 01 · The race in 90 seconds

Godon takes the prologue; Pogačar plays it safe at +7

OPENING3.2 km ITT, Villars-sur-Glâne city-centre. Technical, power-focused, the standard Romandie opener.

UNFOLDSGodon set the reference time; Oliveira and Søderqvist split second on the same gap. Pogačar finished sixth at +7 — Cyclingnews' phrasing: 'overall race favourite Tadej Pogačar finished a distant sixth place, just one second further behind' Oliveira. Deliberately conservative day-one for the team's protected card.

DECIDEDPrologue established positions to one-digit seconds. The race's GC would be settled on the later climbing stages.

FINALEOverall GC trajectory beyond the prologue is partially documented in current public coverage; this page captures the prologue's reliable picture and notes the favourite hierarchy going into the climbing days.
