§ 01 · The stage

Del Toro on Jebel Hafeet

168 km from Al Ain Museum to the Jebel Hafeet summit, the canonical UAE Tour GC day. Del Toro attacked late on the climb; Plapp and Gall could hold for a small front group but couldn't gap him for the line.

The Evenepoel ITT margin from Stage 2 was always exposed on a climbing stage that asks more from the climbers than from the time-triallists. Del Toro retook the leader's jersey on the last selection.
