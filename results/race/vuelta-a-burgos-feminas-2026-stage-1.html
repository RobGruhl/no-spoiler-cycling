§ 01 · The stage

Wiebes gallops to the opening sprint and the first leader's jersey

The opening 127 km circuit stage around Burgos followed the classic sprint-day script. A small early breakaway was allowed up the road on the rolling laps but never given much rope, with SD Worx-Protime riding tempo on the front throughout; the move was reeled in roughly 17.5 km from the line. From there the run-in to the Gamonal finish became a lead-out battle, and SD Worx took it over inside the final kilometres.

The SD Worx train delivered Lorena Wiebes to the front at high speed and she launched early, easily outsprinting Chiara Consonni and Georgia Baker to win by a clear margin. It was a near-perfectly executed team sprint and gave Wiebes both the stage and, with the time bonus, the first purple leader's jersey of the 2026 Vuelta a Burgos Feminas. She started the next stage with a handful of seconds in hand over Consonni and Baker.

§ 02 · GC impact

Wiebes into purple

Wiebes leads the GC after Stage 1 thanks to the stage win and finish bonus, ahead of Consonni and Baker on a few seconds. With three flat-to-hilly stages before the queen stage at Lagunas de Neila, the pure climbers stayed quiet and let the sprinters' teams dictate the opening days.
