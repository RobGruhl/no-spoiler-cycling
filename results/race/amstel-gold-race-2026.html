§ 01 · The race in 90 seconds

Evenepoel's 42-km solo, one second clear of last year's winner

OPENING257.2 km from Maastricht to Berg en Terblijt, the longest Ardennes day. Started at 11:10 CET, finished at 17:12. A long, gradually thinning race that suits the Ardennes set-piece favourites — Evenepoel, Skjelmose, Pogačar (absent), Pidcock (not on the start sheet).

UNFOLDSEvenepoel launched what would become the day's race-winning move 42 km from the line — the kind of long-range commitment that has defined his Classics career. Skjelmose, the defending champion, was the only rider who could follow at first, then was distanced.

DECIDEDThe closing kilometres reduced to a two-up rematch from 2025 (when Skjelmose beat Evenepoel in a sprint). One second separated them this year, with Evenepoel's long solo proving the right tactical choice on the Cauberg-and-after climbing sequence.

FINALECosnefroy chased home for third — the Decathlon engine that has been at the front of every Ardennes race this spring without the leader-class result. Evenepoel's first big win in Red Bull-Bora kit; Skjelmose's runner-up validates Lidl-Trek's Ardennes pivot.

§ 02 · Decisive moments

Where the race tilted

  1. 01 42 km to go Cauberg-area approach
    Evenepoel goes solo
    42 km from the finish, Evenepoel attacked and went alone — committed long-range Classics move. Only Skjelmose could initially follow.
  2. 02 8 km to go Final climbs
    Two-up rematch
    Skjelmose held Evenepoel's wheel through the final climbs but couldn't close the last seconds. The 2025 sprint duel inverted: Evenepoel solo by one.
§ 03 · Team-by-team

Who pressed, who missed

Red Bull - BORA - hansgrohe
Evenepoel's first major in new colours

After the Ronde podium and the Liège third still to come, Amstel was Evenepoel's first major win in Red Bull-Bora-Hansgrohe kit. The 42-km solo is signature.

Lidl-Trek
Skjelmose runner-up in his title defence

Defending champion finishes one second short. The Ardennes block was always going to be the team's most contested terrain this spring; finishing the first one of three by one second is a clean result.

UAE Team Emirates - XRG
Cosnefroy at the front without Pogačar

Pogačar skipped Amstel to recover for Flèche and Liège. Cosnefroy carried the team's Ardennes card and took third — the kind of result that justifies UAE's depth.

§ 06 · Aftermath

First Red Bull-Bora major

Evenepoel's spring at Red Bull-Bora-Hansgrohe had been a slow build — Ronde podium, the long Liège breakaway, Amstel win. The 42-km solo is the move Evenepoel has used to win a hundred different races. It worked again.
