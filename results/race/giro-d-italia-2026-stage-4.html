Stage 4: Catanzaro → Cosenza
Giro d'Italia 2026

Jhonatan Narváez took UAE's first Giro stage win two days after the Stage 2 mass crash, beating Orluis Aular and Giulio Ciccone from a reduced front group after the Cozzo Tunno climb sorted the favourites from the pure sprinters. The pure sprinters' group came in over eight minutes down.

§ 01 · The stage

UAE salvage after the Bulgarian disaster

138 km, Cozzo Tunno climb (14.4 km at 5.9%) summit at ~km 94. The cat-1 climb sorted the favourites from the pure sprinters; the day resolved in a reduced-group sprint. Narváez — UAE's senior all-rounder, on the team for any-terrain stages — took the team's first stage win two days after the Bulgarian descent crash that had wiped out four teammates.

§ 02 · GC impact

GC moves slightly

Magnier loses the leader's jersey to whoever finished in the front of the reduced group; the GC question is still well in advance of the Stage 7 Blockhaus climbing day.
