§ S4 · MOUNTAIN · 195km km

Stage 4: Bourges - Uchon
Paris-Nice 2026

Vingegaard took the mountain finish at Uchon after attacking at 1 km to go, 42 seconds clear of Daniel Martínez and putting the GC question on early terms. Atrocious weather, a mass crash, and Juan Ayuso's race-ending injury made it the spring's most chaotic GC stage to date.

§ 01 · The stage

Vingegaard's first stab; Ayuso's race ends

195 km from Bourges to the Uchon mountain finish in atrocious weather. Vingegaard attacked at 1 km to go and rode away for the stage. A mass crash earlier in the stage ended Juan Ayuso's race; the UAE Tour podium man abandoned injured.

§ 02 · GC impact

First major GC selection. Vingegaard takes the leader's jersey and the GC question is now everyone's response to him.
