§ 01 · The season so far

Arc

Florian Lipowitz arrived in 2026 carrying the weight of a breakout 2025 — a Tour de France podium behind Tadej Pogačar and Jonas Vingegaard had announced the German as Red Bull - BORA - hansgrohe's next Grand Tour leader. But the new season stayed stubbornly winless deep into June, the results solid without a victory to underline his rise. That changed on the queen stage of the Tour of Slovenia, where he attacked on the climb to Vršič, soloed clear over the summit and descended to a stage win in Kranjska Gora ahead of teammate Giulio Pellizzari for a Red Bull one-two. The win — his first of 2026 — also handed him the overall lead with one stage to ride, and landed as a pointed statement of climbing form just two weeks before he returns to the Tour de France as one of the race's genuine podium contenders.
