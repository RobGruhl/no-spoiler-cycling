§ 01 · The season so far

Arc

Christian Scaroni opened 2026 as one of XDS Astana's most prolific early-season performers, winning the overall and a stage at the Tour of Oman, taking the Classica Camp de Morvedre with a late attack, and finishing 2nd at the Tour des Alpes-Maritimes. That aggressive baroudeur instinct carried into the Giro d'Italia, where the Italian repeatedly threw himself into the breakaways on home roads. His standout day came on Stage 11, when he crashed on the Colle di Guaitarola descent yet picked himself up to finish 6th on the stage. His Giro became a hard-luck break-and-attack arc rather than a result-chasing campaign, defined by combativity over classification. As the race rolls toward Rome on 31 May with Vingegaard in pink, Scaroni's season already stands as a career-best for wins and visibility. He looks to keep mining the late-season Italian one-day calendar where he has been a regular animator.
