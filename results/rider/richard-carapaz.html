Tour de France — Stage 10: Aurillac → Le Lioran
GRAND TOUR
STAGE HUNTER P11 +2:09 · EF Education - EasyPost

Carapaz was the heroic protagonist of Stage 10, animating the race with a solo attack on the Puy Mary (Pas de Peyrol) with 6.9 km of the climb remaining. He crested the summit with a 20-second lead and extended to over a minute on the valley floor, carrying that advantage through the Col de Pertus until Pogačar hunted him down 250 metres from the summit. Caught and dropped, Carapaz refused to collapse, finishing 11th at +2:09 — comfortably ahead of all remaining chasers. It was an EF Education - EasyPost moment of genuine ambition on Bastille Day, ultimately eclipsed only by the stage winner's extraordinary power.

GRAND TOUR
STAGE HUNTER P3 +0:02 · EF Education - EasyPost

Carapaz delivers EF Education-EasyPost's best result of this Tour, finishing third on the Les Angles summit at the same time as Vingegaard.
