§ 01 · The season so far

Arc

Felix Gall, Austria's premier stage-race climber, sharpened his 2026 in the spring stage races and signalled big Giro ambitions at the Volta a Catalunya, where he took second on the brutal Coll de Pal queen stage behind a rampant Jonas Vingegaard. That hierarchy repeated all through the Giro d'Italia, where Gall was the clear best of the rest in the mountains — runner-up to Vingegaard on Blockhaus, at Pila, at Carì and on the final climb to Piancavallo. The consistency earned him second overall at +5:22, clear of Jai Hindley in third — the standout Grand Tour result of his career and confirmation of his standing among the world's elite climbers. He carries that form toward the summer's biggest races.
