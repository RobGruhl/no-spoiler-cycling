§ 01 · The season so far

Arc

Jordi Meeus's 2026 ran true to label — the fast finisher who can survive longer Classics, never quite the day's headline but always close enough to remember. The defining vignette of his spring is Inner Ring's Paris-Roubaix race review describing the late chase: 'Only Ganna was thirty seconds behind and closing, towing Jordi Meeus across.' Meeus didn't bridge to the lead duo; he was on the wheel of the rider who came closest. That's the Meeus spring template — sprint into the right group of riders, ride the right wheel, finish in the top ten without winning. Multiple cobbled and semi-classic wins earlier in his career; in 2026 the Red Bull-Bora-Hansgrohe role expects a stage win or a Classics top-five before the year is out. Tour stages in July remain the obvious target.
