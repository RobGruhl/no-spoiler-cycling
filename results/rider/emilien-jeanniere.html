§ 01 · The season so far

Arc

TotalEnergies' fast-finishing one-day specialist, Emilien Jeannière spent the 2026 Flemish spring chasing results in the sprinters' semi-classics. His standout marker so far is fourth at the Danilith Nokere Koerse, where he was the best of the ProTeam fast men in the uphill bunch sprint to Nokere behind WorldTour finishers Philipsen, Meeus and Molano. That positioning on a rising cobbled finish is true to his profile as a puncheur-sprinter who thrives on Belgian one-day terrain. With a programme pointed at races like the Brussels Cycling Classic, the Nokere top-five is the kind of result he'll look to convert into a podium or win as the season's one-day calendar continues.
