§ 01 · The season so far

Arc

Eleonora Camilla Gasparrini took the biggest result of her young career at Milano-Sanremo Donne in 2026, riding to third place on home roads. The UAE Team ADQ rider followed the decisive Poggio selection into a front group of five and, supported by teammate Dominika Włodarczyk's selfless work on the run-in, held off the chasing peloton to round out the podium behind Lotte Kopecky and Noemi Rüegg. A home Monument podium that announced the Italian as one of her squad's emerging one-day cards.
