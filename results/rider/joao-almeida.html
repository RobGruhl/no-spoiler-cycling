Arc

João Almeida opened his 2026 as UAE Team Emirates - XRG's GC leader at the Volta a la Comunitat Valenciana, where he was the best of the rest behind Remco Evenepoel. He was the only rider able to follow Evenepoel on the Stage 4 queen stage to Teulada Moraira, limiting his losses to 24 seconds, and finished second overall by 31 seconds — backed up by team-mate Brandon McNulty in fifth. No stage win, but a steady, authoritative early-season block that confirmed his status as the strongest week-long stage racer in the field behind the eventual winner.
