No Spoiler Cycling is an unofficial fan project. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to the UCI, race organizers, teams, riders, or broadcasters. Race names and trademarks belong to their respective owners and are used only to refer to the events themselves.

Contact

This is a small hobby project. The best way to reach us — for questions, corrections, or anything else — is to open an issue on the project's GitHub issues page. Please avoid posting sensitive personal information in a public issue.

Accessibility

We want the site to be usable by everyone. We aim to meet the basics of WCAG 2.2 Level AA — sufficient color contrast, keyboard-navigable controls, meaningful text alternatives, and content that works without relying on color alone.

Accessibility is ongoing work and some pages will fall short. If you hit a barrier — something you can't read, reach, or operate — please tell us by opening a GitHub issue describing the problem and the page, and we will do our best to fix it.

Takedown & rights holders

We build on publicly available information and intend to use race names and factual data fairly. If you are a rights holder and believe something on this site infringes your rights or should be removed, we want to hear from you and will respond promptly.

  • Open an issue on the GitHub issues page describing the material and the page it appears on; or
  • Use GitHub's DMCA takedown process for a formal notice.

Please identify the specific content and your relationship to the rights so we can act quickly.
