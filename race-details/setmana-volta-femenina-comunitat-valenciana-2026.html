Setmana
Volta Femenina Comunitat Valenciana

The Setmana Ciclista Volta Femenina de la Comunitat Valenciana is a four-stage race through Spain's eastern Valencia region. The race typically features a mix of sprint stages and hilly terrain with categorized climbs that can shake up the general classification. Key summit finishes like the Alto de Barx have decided past editions, making this an important early-season test for all-rounders.

§ 06 — Historical Context

Form book & lore

The Setmana Ciclista Volta Valenciana is one of Spain's most important women's stage races, providing an early-season racing opportunity in the warm Mediterranean climate. The race attracts a mix of WorldTour teams and Spanish continental squads, creating entertaining racing dynamics.

§ 07 — Viewing Notes

When to tune in

The hilly stages with summit finishes like Alto de Barx typically decide the overall classification. Sprint stages can see echelon action in crosswinds along the coast. Watch for attacks on the categorized climbs - gaps established here can be decisive.
